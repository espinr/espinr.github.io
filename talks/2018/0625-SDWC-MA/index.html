Activ-IoTy

Sports Timekeeping and Management System

  • To promote healthy and competitive sports
  • Offers challenging personal incentives to engage runners
  • Enabling emergency features
  • Compatible with third-party applications (e.g., social networks)

Athletes + Checkpoints

Low-cost Open IoT Platform

  • Based on RFID
  • Easy to develop and deploy
  • Open Data compliant (open, standard formats)
